Weight-Painting

Cannot switch bones in weight paint mode in Blender 2.8 !

In object mode Select armature > and shift select mesh

How can I hide parts of the mesh in Weightpaint mode?

Select the outer part of your model in Edit Mode and hit H to hide them . Then Ctrl-Tab into Weight Paint and then hit F to enter Face Select Mode and the parts hidden previously in Edit Mode will disappear .
